## SEO + UX Notes

This structure optimizes for:

1. **E-E-A-T (Google ranking signals):** About the Author section builds trust; structured schema markup confirms expertise
2. **Featured snippets:** PAUL's Quick Read and condensed LevelUp are prime candidates for Google's "People also ask" boxes
3. **Dwell time:** Table of Contents + related posts + CTAs keep readers on-site longer (SEO signal)
4. **Scannability:** Headings, bold text, lists make content skimmable (reduces bounce rate)
5. **Internal linking:** Multiple CTAs to /services and other blog posts improve site crawlability
6. **Mobile UX:** Responsive design, clear hierarchy, short paragraphs on small screens
